Why Land Use Matters

Land-use decisions are among the most important choices local governments make. They influence affordability, mobility, private property, public services, taxes, business development, and quality of life.

My approach is practical: growth should be planned, infrastructure should matter, local conditions should be respected, and policy should be judged by real-world impacts on residents and communities.
